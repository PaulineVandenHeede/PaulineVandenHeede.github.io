About me

Hi my name is Pauline!

I'm a game development student from Belgium with a passion for graphics programming. Currently I'm looking for a company to do my end of study internship, starting February 2023. In my portfolio tab you can find a few projects I worked on myself and that define me more as a programmer, but also show many of my good qualities.

Outisde of programming I have a lot of other passions too, one of these is sports. On most moments in the year you can find me preparing for another challenge that is coming my way and in summer you can find me hiking through the mountains. As one of my next goals, I want to be running half a marathon next spring but also ride the tour of flanders again!

Outside of sports, reading books is another favourite past time of me. I love getting swept up in the imaginary world of a writer now and then. Not only do I read fiction, I read a lot of self help books as well to continue improving myself. As I try to improve myself constantly, I never stop learning this is why I recently started learning Italian.
