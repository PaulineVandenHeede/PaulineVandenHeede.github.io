Global illumination

Abstract

Producing an image that simulates the effect of global illumination with a relative small amount of noise is a difficult task when it comes to having relatively short render times. It states the need for knowing the effectiveness of variance reduction techniques. Variance reduction techniques try to reduce the noise in the image without having to increase the amount of samples the rendering algorithm needs to produce an image with relatively little noise. The technique that will be used for the rendering algorithm is a combination of the Monte Carlo integration and path tracing. Then the variance reduction techniques, stratified and importance sampling, will be introduced. At the end it will be proven that these variance reduction techniques are effective in rendering the noise in images without needing extra samples and having a similar render time.
